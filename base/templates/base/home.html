Welcome to Bioinformatics Study Site

Hello everyone, I am glad that you decided to explore this site. Bioinformatics is all about designing methods and tools for studying and understanding biological data. This site provides a discussion and study platform for those who already have an understanding of bioinformatics, as well as those who want to learn about it. All you need is to sign up and create a study room with topics and or join already created rooms by commenting.

To sign up, click on the login button with passport icon on the right corner and sign up. Once you have signed up you will be redirected to the homepage where you can create, comment and edit your rooms and messages. Thanks and all the best.
